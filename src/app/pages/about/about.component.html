Welcome to New Mashareq Ltd, a leading contracting company dedicated to delivering exceptional construction services. With a strong track record of successful projects, we take pride in our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

At New Mashareq Ltd, we specialize in a wide range of construction services, including commercial, residential, industrial, and infrastructure projects. Our team of skilled professionals, including engineers, architects, and project managers, bring a wealth of expertise and experience to every project we undertake.

What sets us apart is our unwavering dedication to excellence. We prioritize quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and adherence to project timelines and budgets. From the initial planning and design stages to the final execution and completion, we ensure that every aspect of your project is handled with the utmost care and precision.

Our comprehensive range of services includes:

  • Renovation and Remodeling: We specialize in transforming existing spaces, breathing new life into them with innovative designs and high-quality craftsmanship
  • Infrastructure Development: Our capabilities extend to infrastructure projects, including roads, bridges, utilities, and other critical public works.
  • Project Management: With our experienced project management team, we ensure effective coordination, cost control, and efficient resource allocation for successful project outcomes.
